About the artist

Welcome! I'm Austin, a professional S.C. portrait artist of 10+ years. I'm happy to answer any questions you may have about my process or pricing. The easiest way to reach me is on Instagram @sketchyactivity or via email at austinsketches@gmail.com. I am not currently open to commissions.

To keep things moderately cheesy, here's one of my favorite quotes from ya boy HT Thoreau:

We must learn to reawaken and keep ourselves awake, not by mechanical aids, but by an infinite expectation of the dawn, which does not forsake us even in our soundest sleep. I know of no more encouraging fact than the unquestionable ability of man to elevate his life by a conscious endeavour. It is something to be able to paint a particular picture, or to carve a statue, and so to make a few objects beautiful; but it is far more glorious to carve and paint the very atmosphere and medium through which we look, which morally we can do. Commission Rules

brush

What I Will Draw

  • People (my favorite!) - Ideally 1-2 people in a given drawing. If you want a larger group photo drawn, I recommend opting for a digital drawing rather than a traditional ballpoint drawing. If you prefer traditional ballpoint for a large group portrait, you will need to opt for a larger size (like 17x22in or larger) to accommodate the detail required for multiple subjects.
  • Animals
block

What I Won't Draw

  • Explicit content, hate symbols
  • Fantasy & cartoon characters
  • Landscapes, buildings, cars, or other non-portrait subjects
timer

Turnaround Time

Turnaround time is typically 2-5 days for single-person 8x10 or digital portraits. Expect longer delays if requesting a drawing of more people!

attach_file

How do you get the drawing?

For traditional drawings, I will send you the drawing in the mail! I cover shipping üòä. For digital drawings, I use Procreate, and I'll send you a high-resolution image file so that you have the ability to print the drawing on a size and material of your choice. I do not pay for printing of digital drawings.

attach_money
